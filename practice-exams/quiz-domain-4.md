**Q1. (D4.1) A model trained on job application data from a male-dominated industry shows a lower hiring prediction score for female candidates, regardless of their qualifications. This is an example of what type of bias?**
A. Algorithmic Bias
B. Historical Bias
C. Measurement Bias
D. Interpretability Bias

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer and Explanation</strong></summary>
**Answer:** B. Historical Bias
**Explanation:** Historical bias occurs when the data reflects and perpetuates existing real-world biases (like historical gender imbalance in a workforce) into the model.
</details>

**Q2. (D4.1) Which AWS service is used to create a Human-in-the-Loop workflow to send low-confidence predictions from an AI model to a human reviewer for validation and quality improvement?**
A. Amazon Comprehend
B. Amazon SageMaker Clarify
C. Amazon Augmented AI (A2I)
D. AWS CloudTrail

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer and Explanation</strong></summary>
**Answer:** C. Amazon Augmented AI (A2I)
**Explanation:** A2I is the managed service for integrating human review into ML workflows to improve model fairness, confidence, and accuracy.
</details>

**Q3. (D4.2) The degree to which a human can understand and trust the outputs of an AI model, specifically the rationale behind a decision, is defined as:**
A. Transparency
B. Accountability
C. Explainability (XAI)
D. Veracity

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer and Explanation</strong></summary>
**Answer:** C. Explainability (XAI)
**Explanation:** Explainability (or Interpretability) is the concept of justifying an AI model's output to a human user. Transparency relates to open documentation and licensing.
</details>

**Q4. (D4.2) Which Amazon SageMaker feature helps increase model transparency by documenting the model's intended use, performance metrics, and fairness assessments?**
A. SageMaker Studio
B. SageMaker Model Monitor
C. SageMaker Model Cards
D. SageMaker Clarify

<details>
<summary><strong>Answer and Explanation</strong></summary>
**Answer:** C. SageMaker Model Cards
**Explanation:** Model Cards are the tool within SageMaker used to document a model's lifecycle and characteristics, promoting transparency and governance.
</details>
