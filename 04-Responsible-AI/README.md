# ⚖️ Domain 4: Responsible AI (14% Exam Weight)

This domain covers the critical principles and practices needed to ensure AI systems are fair, transparent, and accountable.

## Files in this Domain:

* **`4.1-Ethics-and-Bias.md`**: Details the principles of FAT (Fairness, Accountability, Transparency), identifies different types of bias (e.g., Historical Bias), and discusses mitigation strategies using **Amazon Augmented AI (A2I)**.

## Key Focus Areas:

* The definition and sources of **Model Bias** (especially Data Bias).
* The role of the **Human-in-the-Loop** (A2I) in mitigating risk.
* The importance of **Explainability (XAI)** in AI decision-making.
